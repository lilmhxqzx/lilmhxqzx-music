ABOUT ME

🎵 LOST IN MY OWN WORLD 🎵

🎤 THE STORY

I'm Lilmhxqzx, a musician who channels pain into sound. My music is a reflection of real emotions, real struggles, and the rawness of what it means to be lost in your own world. Every track tells a story of heartbreak, resilience, and the endless search for meaning.

This is not just music—it's a confession. It's therapy. It's survival.

🎸 THE SOUND

Nu metal is my voice. It's heavy, it's honest, and it doesn't apologize. Combining aggressive guitars with introspective lyrics, I create music that hits hard but heals deeper. Every riff, every beat, every word is a weapon against the silence.

This is what pain sounds like.

💔 THE MISSION

To make people feel less alone in their darkness. To show that vulnerability is strength. To prove that broken hearts can create beautiful things. Music is my language when words fail, and I want to be your voice when yours breaks.
